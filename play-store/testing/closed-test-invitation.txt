I am looking for closed testers for LocalMD Reader, an Android Markdown viewer.

Purpose:
Confirm the first Google Play build and collect feedback before public release.

Request:
- Opt in from the Google Play testing link.
- Stay opted in for 14 days.
- Open a Markdown file if possible.
- Send brief feedback if you notice anything.

App details:
- Local Markdown viewer.
- No ads.
- No login.
- No network permission.
- Files are rendered on your device.

Please check:
- The app installs.
- The app launches.
- .md and .markdown files open.
- The document is readable.
- Tabs, theme switching, and pinch font sizing work.

You do not need to send private Markdown files. If you report an issue, avoid
including personal information.
